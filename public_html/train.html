Training



Leveling up your character is one of the most important things you
have to do in Dragon Ball Z: Chronicles. Energy Barrier and suppress
are your friends when it comes to training. Although, some races dont
have barrier, suppress can still work without it. Suppress lowers your
powerlevel to a number that you desire. This will enable you to gain
the most powerlevel by each hit to a mob. But becareful, if you suppress
too low, you can get killed by such said mob. When you reach certain
points of your power. There will be some limits, that are able to be
broken thanks to Chikara. To train chikara, you need to visit one of
the fighting schools after you have reached 5 million powerlevel.

In these schools, you either will be taught the ways of evil or good.
Remember, some clans are aligned based, so be sure that the clan you
want to join, accepts the align your going for. Weighted Equipment is
very good when it comes to gravity training and mob training. Weighted
eq can be found around the swamps of Planet Earth, it will increase
how much powerlevel you gain, since your training under extra weight and
making it harder to move. When you lose, you will experience a powerlevel
loss, even though its not that much, while you are maxed, it can be
alot. Biodroids will lose biomass and half the powerlevel that other
races lose when they die.
