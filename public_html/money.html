Making Money



Making money is probably one of the easiest things to do on the mud.
It is recommended to wait for you to reach the max, which is 100 million
powerlevel. So you can start going around and killing mobs that contain
alot of gold. Also, you can bank the money and wait for it to gain interests.
Another tricky way to gain money, is to auction good equipment that people
want. The best equipment that goes on auction, is probably renamed equipment
or clan equipment. Also, you can gather the dragonballs that are scattered
around earth and wish for wealth.
