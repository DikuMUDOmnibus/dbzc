Buus

Description:
Buus, also known as golems in certain circles, are creatures fashioned
entirely by magic. Inmense power courses through their notably rubbery
bodies, which characterizes all Buus. They have an inate appetite that
never seems to be satisfied, neither by food or others beings. Their
bodies allow them to assimilate anything they wish to absorb into their
DNA, making them extremely versatile and dangerous opponents. It's said
that Buus still retain some of the abilities of the original Buu, or those
he stole anyway. They feed off the lifeforce in matter, known to them as
kili, which seems to power and trigger their transformations. Strangely
enough, the way in which they evolve is molded by their personalities,
a trait never seen on a race before.
