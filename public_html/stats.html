Character Attributes

Attributes are that what makes your character unique and special.
It is what separates the strong from the weak. You may choose for your
character to have more brain than brawn, or visa versa. When you train your
character you may have to sacrifice one thing for another. For example you
may want your character to be incredibly strong, but you might have to be
a little slow.


There are only seven attributes:

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STRENGTH (STR) - Affects the damage done with melee and energy attacks to target.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INTELLIGENCE (INT) - Affects the defense from melee and energy attacks from target.
- Determines the speed of learning new skills.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DEXTERITY (DEX) - How accurate attacks are and how well you can avoid one.
- Determines how many items you can carry.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONDITION (CON) - Determines how much energy you obtain per gain.
- Affects your Life/Energy heal rate.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WISDOM (WIS) - Ability to learn skills quickly and to their full potential.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CHARISMA (CHA) - Improves leadership in groups.
- Helps with being a good speaker of the people.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LUCK (LCK) - Will determine how much luck you have in the heat of combat.
- Determines how many times you will hit critically an enemy.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can train each attribute with the train command: train [attribute]
You can also see your current attributes with the score command. You can also
see how much attribute training sessions you have with the practice command.
