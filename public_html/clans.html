Clans


-Cobra Sect
Cobra Sect has been considered one of the most deadliest clans in
the Universe. Most of them are trained well in the arts of killing
and can probably handle highers odds.

-Z Fighters
The Z Fighters are an elite group of warriors that keep the peace
and balance through-out the whole world. Most of them are well trained
in the arts of fighting, but they tend to use diplomacy first.

-Majin Dynasty
The Majins are a group of mercenaries, well trained assassins that
will won't kill you unless you have a bounty on your head, or someone
else wants you dead. These mercenaries can well take on many opponents
at the same time. Even though they are a small sized group, they tend
to fight uneven odds, and even come out victorious.
