Equipment (EQ)



Another important aspect of DragonBall Z: Chronicles is the equipment.
Many people would kill to have an unique piece in the entire MUD. More
if its better than everything else. Apart from making you appear cool
to each person that looks at you. Each piece of equipment gives you an
extra armor or attack %. If your the best equipped character, you should
probably know that you won't get hit as hard as someone that has barely
no good equipment. Choosing the kind of equipment you should use is easy,
just pay attention to where you can use the equipment and the % it carries.
Clans have most of the best equipment of the MUD, making them raid each
other headquarters for pieces of the equipment.

In addition, androids can wear chips into their cpu, making their powerlevel
and stat mods increase. All the equipment has powerlevel boundaries, if your
PL is not within those boundaries, you won't be able to wear it, you can check
this by typing exam [equipment].
