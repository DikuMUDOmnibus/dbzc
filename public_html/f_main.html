Description:

Enter the world of Dragon Ball Z: Chaotic Chronicles. Immerse yourself with our variety of
races to use. Design your ultimate fighting character along with the different races
that will make your character unique. Collect the dragonballs that are scattered around
the Earth, and wish upon the dragon. Conquer the different planets around the universe,
turn them into your colonies, and make them collect money for your clan. Have clan
battles and raids through the planets or even the enemies's headquarters. Fly through the
galaxy exploring new horizons as you'll find new and exciting stuff yet to come.
