FAQ [Frequently Asked Questions]

1)  What is Chikara?
     Type help chikara. In short, Chikara
     is a technique used so that you can go beyond
     the limits of your power.

2)  How do I enter a spaceship?
     Type: Enter spaceship [ship name]

3)  How do I leave a spaceship?
     Type: Leave spaceship

4)  What are schools and what are they for?
     It is where you recieve an alignment and where you can learn
     the technique known as Chikara.

5)  Why doesn't my skills percentages reach 100%?
     You need WISDOM at 25 for them to reach 100%

6)  How do I check my Buu or character status?
     Type: Kili and/or Status

6)  What is Biomass?
     It is a substance Biodroids absorb off their dead victims
     that allows them to transform into other stages. (Help Stage or Biomass)

7)  I have reached 5 million PL, what now?
     You must spacetravel to a alignment school where you will learn Chikara.

7)  How do I absorb PL/Kili/Biomass?
     All of them are absorbed differently. PL is absorbed
     with the absorb skill. Biomass is absorbed automatically
     upon death of the victim. And Kili is absorbed by using
     absorption when your victim is near death. All of these
     types of absorbs are race dependant.

8)  How many attribute training sessions will I gain?
     You gain 84 stats in total. [Incluiding the 4 at the start]

9)  How many ki gains will I recieve?
     You recieve 315 ki gains in total.

10)  How do I gravity train?
      Help gravity, in short, go to a gravity chamber
      type gravset [#] (1 for each 10k pl you have) then,
      type train gravity.

11)  How often can I gravity train?
      Help gravity, in short, go to a gravity chamber
      type gravset [#] (1 for each 10k pl you have) then,
      type train gravity.

15)  What is Boost work?
      Boost is a gift given to the currently first person
      that connected to the MUD. It gives additional PL
      gaining on mobs and gravity training.

16)  What is Boost work?
      Search = lets you search your surroundings for close objects.
      Chargeto = Its a command that lets you fly towards your enemy and start an attack.
      Survey = allows you to see closeby landmarks.

17)  What is popsupport and how does it work?
      Planets are controlled by clans. A clan can take over a planet
      if the population support towards that clan is low enough for
      a government change. While propaganding to lower a clan's support
      you CAN be pked on spot. After the popsupport has lowered enough
      you can type capture to make the planet your clans.
