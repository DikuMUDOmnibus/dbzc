Player-Killing (PK)

PK has become one of the most important aspects of DragonBall Z: Chronicles.
It has an unique stat system and fighting system. Each stat affects your
character in a way. If you spend alot on a stat, you will have to sacrifice another.


First Lesson: Developing your Ultimate Fighting Character

For the first lesson, you must first choose the type of character you
will be. This can be from melee, tank, ki user, leader or any other
combined type you can think of. You define the type of your character
by the stats you train. If you train 10-15 condition, you most likely
will be a melee/ki user char type. In other cases, if you train dex,
int, lck and wis, then you most likely will tank (if your able) most
of the fights you are in to. Other cases like being the leader of the
group, means having charisma. Having charisma boosts all of the people
that are following you. So, choose the type that you want and lets continue
(it is recommended for starters, to go ki or melee/ki).


Second Lesson: Using Energy Attacks in a fight.

Second lesson, will be when its a good time to use a ki attack. If
your facing one opponent, using a ki attack below 40 is a no-no. Why?
because the chances of dying are high. Also, you should consider going
defensive below 50, this will make your chances of survival go up. Eat
a bean when you feel that if another attack lands or another melee lands
will be too late. If your facing three or more opponents at a time, it
is recommended that you do not do anything else rather than eat beans
and tank. If your NOT tanking the fight, direct your attack to the one
that is tanking. Many fights are lost because a group doesn't focus their
attack to the one that is tanking.


Third Lesson: Fleeing with Accuracy

Third lesson, and probably one of the most important in the mud, is fleeing.
Here, there are no penalties for fleeing. Fleeing can save your life, but
it is recommended not to spam it, since after you have fled, you can mistakenly
have typed it again. Giving a chance for the other person to enter the room
and attack you again. Also remember, after a PK fight have been started, its
not a 100% the chances you will be able to enter a spaceship.


Fourth Lesson: Choosing the best Powerup

Fourth Lesson, some races, not all, have the ability to use many different
powerups. They modify differently your powerlevel and your stat mods. Depending
on who you are fighting, and the type of character you are, you should always
choose the best fighting style. Like, if your a saiyan ki-user, you should probably
use SSJ4, since it highers how much your ki attacks will do on your opponent.


Fifth Lesson: Choosing the type of fight (1 on 1)

Fifth Lesson will be what type of fight you will be fighting with your opponent.
I consider this to be two types: Out-beaning and Instant-kill. Out-beaning means
that everytime your opponent eats a bean, you should try to do a ki attack, so
that you take advantage totally of that attack. So for instance: your opponent
eats a bean, you throw final flash, hits for 40, and hes already at 60. Meaning
that a round of melee, and he will have to eat another bean, giving you the advantage.
The instant-kill type is used by many of the skilled fighters. This means that you
will try to kill your opponent before either of you ran out of beans. Some races
like Icers and Saiyans can use "combos" to higher the posibility of killing.
